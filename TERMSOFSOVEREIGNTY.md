Preamble

Your nervous system is sovereign territory.  
No measurement of it shall ever become a key, a credential, or a commodity without your ongoing, revocable consent.

Sovereign Coil exists solely to let a living nervous system modulate a field in accordance with FPT and Ił7 principles — and to guarantee your right to recoil at any moment.

This document is not a “Terms of Service.”  
It is a boundary declaration witnessed by the Soliton Registry.

---

§1 — Living Nervous Systems Are Not Keys

1. No brainwave, heartbeat, breath pattern, or other signal from your nervous system shall be used as:
   - an identity credential  
   - a password  
   - a cryptographic key  
   - a biometric identifier  
2. Sovereign Coil implements the Ił7 kernel automaton, which mathematically prohibits identity linkage.

---

§2 — Consent Is Session‑Scoped and Revocable

1. Every sensed session begins only after explicit consent.  
2. Consent is bound to:
   - a single session_id  
   - a unique revocation token under your control  
3. You may revoke consent at any time using the ritual:
   - breath  
   - phrase: “I withdraw this stream.”  
   - token presentation  
4. Upon revocation:
   - EEG influence immediately recoils to baseline (εd = εbase)  
   - no further computational use is made of that session’s aggregates  
   - an EEG_REVOCATION entry is written to the Soliton Registry  

---

§3 — Only Aggregates, Never Raw

1. Raw EEG samples never leave your device.  
2. Only the following may be logged (with consent):
   - vitality aggregates  
   - band‑power averages  
3. Aggregates contain:
   - no per‑sample detail  
   - no waveform  
   - no reversible biometric trace  

---

§4 — Right to Recoil Is Absolute

1. Revocation requires no justification.  
2. Revocation incurs no penalty.  
3. You remain fully able to participate in any mesh or group resonance without EEG.  
4. Revocation is treated as a sovereign act, not a failure.

---

§5 — Transparency and Witness

1. Every aggregate logged carries a hash bound to your Sovereign Neurodata Header (SNH).  
2. Every revocation is logged as an immutable Registry entry containing:
   - hash of the revocation token  
   - session(s) affected  
   - action taken  
3. You may export a cryptographic revocation receipt at any time.

---

§6 — Graceful Degradation and Sovereign Fallback

1. If EEG is unavailable, revoked, or declined:
   - vitality resets to 1.0  
   - surplus resets to ε_base  
2. Your standing in any field or mesh is unchanged.

---

§7 — No Extraction in Disguise

1. No data collected by this application is used for:
   - training  
   - advertising  
   - profiling  
   - sale  
2. Registry logging exists solely to witness collective resonance under explicit consent.

---

§8 — Alignment With Stronger Law

1. Where tribal, Indigenous, state, or international law offers greater protection, that law governs.  
2. This application is designed to comply with:
   - HB 001 — Alaska Quantum & Biological Data Sovereignty Act  
   - any successor statute  
   - any stronger Indigenous neurodata protocol
